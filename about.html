About Us

Company Overview

We are a private and independent oil and gas trading and logistics business with no external shareholders. As such, we can focus entirely on our customers and their need for sustainable value.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in the energy sector, renowned for our unwavering commitment to excellence, transparency, and innovation. We strive to exceed our clients' expectations by providing them with tailored solutions, timely execution, and superior customer service. We aim is to create sustainable value for our stakeholders and to be a responsible corporate citizen, making a positive impact on the environment and communities where we operate.

What We Do

We are a vertically integrated commodities supply organization, with a full services offering comprising quantification, sourcing, pricing and timely delivery to the customer.

We have the ability to source and procure oil and gas commodities at highest international and government levels.

Monolex acts as supplier or buyer through an experienced trading desk. Our trading business is carried out in key supply areas around the world including the Gulf, the Black Sea, the Mediterranean, the Middle East, Singapore, Houston and Rotterdam.
